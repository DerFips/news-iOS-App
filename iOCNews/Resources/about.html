FeedDeck is a personal feed reader. You do not have to tell anyone else what you like to read. The app does not check with any servers to customize the content. Please visit peterandlinda.com for more information about FeedDeck and for how to get help.

How to add feeds

  1. Tap the Add (+) button and type or paste the address (URL) of the feed (RSS or Atom) you want to add.
  2. Import subscription lists in OPML-format. Most desktop feed readers can export feeds in this format. Use an .opml or .xml file extension. Transfer the exported file to your device, for example as a mail attachment. Use the "Open In..." feature and select FeedDeck.

    Tip: You can export your FeedDeck subscriptions by tapping on Email Support below.

How to use

FeedDeck uses swipe and pull gestures for most features.

Pull down on the list of feeds to refresh all feeds at once.

Tap on a feed to show the list of articles.

Pull down on the list of articles to refresh only this feed.

Tap the Check button to mark all articles as read. Articles will also be marked as read as you scroll down the list.

Use a swipe gesture to return to the feed list.

Tap on an article to view the entire summary of the article.

Tap on the title of the article or the link at the bottom of the summary to see the web version of an article.

Use a swipe gesture to return to the article list.

Tap the Edit button to delete or rearrange feeds. You can also tap on the feed name to edit the name, update the feed address, or enable reader mode.
